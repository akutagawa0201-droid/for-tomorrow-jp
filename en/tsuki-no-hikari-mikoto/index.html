About the Song この曲について

"Tsuki no Hikari" (Moonlight) is a gentle Japanese-style Lo-Fi ballad sung by Mikoto. It captures the quiet feeling of a long-overdue night with a drink — that soft moment when the day fades away and you finally let yourself breathe.

After a busy day, when you simply want to relax without thinking. When you want to cherish your time alone. When you wish to ease your heart just a little — this song is for those nights. Soft female vocals, warm Lo-Fi beats, quiet piano, and a faint Japanese atmosphere come together to gently embrace a tired heart.
