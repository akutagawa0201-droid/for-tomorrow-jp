About the Song この曲について

"Midnight Velvet" is a Japanese City Pop track sung by Mikoto — the second chapter of a late-night city series that began with the previous song, "Steal the Night."

It is 3AM on a deserted urban highway. You leave yesterday behind in the rearview mirror and let the car carry you through the quiet hours before dawn. Mikoto's husky, soulful voice draws you deep into the velvet of the city night, layered over a heavy, rolling bassline and a steady chill beat. Perfect for focused work, late-night drives, or those sleepless nights when you simply want the city to keep you company.
